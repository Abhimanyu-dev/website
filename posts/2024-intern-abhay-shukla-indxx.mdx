## Insights on the Selection Process
The company directly selected students based on resume and CPI without conducting any technical test. There were three people in technical test (one HR, a technical expert and one was senior person from the company). The interview was comparatively easy as compared to my earlier experience and consist of few aptitude questions, basic HR questions and some coding questions. The interview was around 20-25 minutes and all the interviewers were very friendly.

## Insights on the Interview Process
A good resume is must for the companies, try to have at least 1 or 2 very good projects and try to maintain a CPI of 7+ as it will be enough for shortlisting in almost all companies. You just have to be confident during interview as difficulty level of questions in interview is less.
