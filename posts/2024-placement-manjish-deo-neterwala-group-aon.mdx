##  Insights on the interview process
Only one round of interview with HR of the company.

## Sample Interview Questions? 
HR questions were asked like q- tell me about yourself? q- What are your strengths and weaknesses? q- Tell me about an experience when you faced difficulty at work while working on a project? q- How do you handle stress, pressure, and anxiety? q- explain a situation where you thought of a creative solution? q- situation like the team was distressed, so what steps did you follow to reach your goal? q- short and long term goals? Questions related to intern and position of responsibilities.

## Advice for students (do's and don'ts)
- Do not waste summers. - You must be well versed with each and every word in your resume. Have stories to better communicate with the interviewer. - The December time will be overwhelming, have good friends by your side. - Just enjoy the time with the interviewer. - Low cpi guys (i was one too P) won't be able to get enough shortlists, make each and every interview count. Learn SQL, python, Power BI and add some projects regarding the same, will be very very helpful to get a shortlist. - EE, CE, ME, Chemical - make one resume for core too.
